## 2. Complete User Flow

### First-Time User Journey

```
1. SPLASH SCREEN (2 seconds)
   - Beautiful animated card back
   - "Quantum Tarot" title fades in
   - Subtle particle effects (quantum theme)

2. WELCOME SCREEN
   - "Welcome, seeker."
   - Brief explanation (3 sentences max):
     * "Genuine quantum randomness creates truly unique readings"
     * "Each session is personalized to your energy"
     * "Drawing on ancient wisdom and modern psychology"
   - CTA: "Begin Your Journey"

3. NAME & BIRTHDAY
   - "What name shall we call you?"
   - Optional: "Your pronouns?" (she/her, he/him, they/them, other/prefer not to say)
   - "When were you born?" (For astrological integration)
   - Beautiful date picker, mystical design

4. ATTUNEMENT QUESTIONS
   - Title: "Let's Attune to Your Energy"
   - Subtitle: "10 brief questions to personalize your experience"
   - Progress bar at top
   - One question per screen (not overwhelming)
   - Beautiful bubble selection or sliders
   - Questions adapt based on reading type they'll select next

5. READING TYPE SELECTION
   - "What aspect of life calls to you?"
   - Beautiful cards/tiles for each type:
     * Career (briefcase icon, gold)
     * Romance (heart, pink/red)
     * Wellness (lotus, green)
     * Family (tree, brown)
     * Self-Growth (spiral, purple)
     * School (book, blue)
     * General (cosmos, silver)
     * Surprise Me (sparkles, rainbow)

6. SPREAD SELECTION
   - Show available spreads (lock premium ones for free users)
   - Visual representation of each spread
   - Single Card, 3-Card, Celtic Cross (premium), etc.

7. INTENTION SETTING
   - "Take a moment. What question or intention do you hold?"
   - Text input (optional but encouraged)
   - "This focuses the quantum field to your need"
   - Beautiful zen-like screen with breathing animation

8. CARD DRAWING ANIMATION
   - CRITICAL: This must be BEAUTIFUL and feel MYSTICAL
   - Particle effects representing quantum collapse
   - Cards slowly fade/materialize
   - Each card draws individually with pause between
   - Satisfying sound design (optional, toggle-able)

9. READING DISPLAY
   - One card at a time, swipe to see next
   - OR: All cards visible, tap to expand
   - Position name at top
   - Card image (beautiful art)
   - Card name
   - Interpretation in user's adaptive voice
   - Clean, readable typography

10. READING SUMMARY
    - Overview of all cards
    - "Your Synthesis" - Overarching message
    - "Carried Forward" - One key insight to remember
    - Share button (to social, or beautiful image generation)
    - Save to Journal (premium feature)

11. POST-READING SCREEN
    - For free users: "Next reading available in [countdown]"
    - Gentle ad placement (if free)
    - "Upgrade to Mystic for unlimited readings"
    - "Reflect on this reading" (journal prompt - premium)
```

### Returning User Journey

```
1. HOME SCREEN
   - "Welcome back, [Name]"
   - Current cosmic weather (optional feature: "Today's Energy: [tarot card of day]")
   - Quick access: "New Reading"
   - Reading History (premium)
   - Journal (premium)
   - Settings

2. NEW READING
   - Remembers previous reading type, asks "Same as last time or different?"
   - Quick path for regular users
   - Same flow as above from step 5
```

## 3. Design Aesthetics (5 Profiles)

### 3A. Minimal Modern (Gen Z Analytical)
**Color Palette:**
- Background: Pure black (#000000) or off-white (#FAFAFA)
- Primary: Gold (#D4AF37)
- Accent: Cool white (#FFFFFF)
- Text: High contrast

**Typography:**
- Sans-serif, clean (Inter, SF Pro)
- Hierarchy through size, not decoration

**Visual Style:**
- Flat design
- Line art for cards
- Minimal animation
- Lots of white/black space
- Geometric shapes

**Example Cards:** Line art, gold foil effect on black

---

### 3B. Soft Mystical (Millennial Intuitive)
**Color Palette:**
- Background: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA)
- Primary: Rose gold (#B76E79)
- Accent: Dusty blue (#9DB4C0)
- Tertiary: Cream (#FFFDD0)

**Typography:**
- Serif for headers (Playfair Display)
- Sans for body (Montserrat Light)

**Visual Style:**
- Gradients and glows
- Watercolor textures
- Dreamy blur effects
- Soft shadows
- Organic shapes

**Example Cards:** Watercolor art style, soft edges

---

### 3C. Bold Authentic (Gen Z Intuitive)
**Color Palette:**
- Background: Deep purple (#2E1A47)
- Primary: Electric blue (#00FFF5)
- Accent: Hot pink (#FF006E)
- Tertiary: Neon yellow (#FFBE0B)

**Typography:**
- Bold sans-serif (Archivo Black)
- Body: Grotesque sans

**Visual Style:**
- High contrast
- Neon effects
- Sharp edges
- Bold graphics
- Y2K/cyberspace vibes

**Example Cards:** Modern digital art, bold colors

---

### 3D. Elegant Classic (Traditional/Older Millennial)
**Color Palette:**
- Background: Navy (#1A1A2E)
- Primary: Antique gold (#C9B037)
- Accent: Deep red (#8B0000)
- Tertiary: Ivory (#FFFFF0)

**Typography:**
- Serif (Cormorant Garamond)
- Traditional, elegant

**Visual Style:**
- Art deco influences
- Gold foil effects
- Ornate borders
- Classic tarot imagery
- Sophisticated animations

**Example Cards:** Traditional Rider-Waite style, gold accents

---

### 3E. Witchy Earthy (Alternative Spiritual)
**Color Palette:**
- Background: Forest green (#0B3D0B)
- Primary: Terracotta (#E07A5F)
- Accent: Sage green (#81B29A)
- Tertiary: Bone white (#F2F4F3)

**Typography:**
- Handwritten-style headers (Satisfy)
- Natural sans body (Lato)

**Visual Style:**
- Organic textures (wood, stone, plants)
- Hand-drawn elements
- Earthy, grounded
- Botanical illustrations
- Crystals, moon phases

**Example Cards:** Botanical tarot style, earthy tones

- Subtle constellation patterns in background
- Card backs that shimmer/glow slightly
